About Me

I am an assistant professor in the department of mathematical sciences at the University of Texas at Dallas. I got my Ph.D. in mathematics from the University of Toronto in 2018 under the supervision of Dr. Alex Nabutovsky and Dr. Hau-Tieng Wu. I was an assistant research professor at Duke University where I worked with Dr. David Dunson and Dr. Hau-Tieng Wu. My research focuses on manifold learning and its applications.
